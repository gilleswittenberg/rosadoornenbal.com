Hunnybunlookingforsculpture, 2014
Single Channel, HD Video loop, sound, 3.06 min. Film stills.

info

    In the video that is part of a diptych with Monumentals, a different attitude towards making is introduced. In an abstract visual journey ‘hunnybunlookingforsculpture’ shows the exploration of a young lady, fantasizing about shape, form and material.
