Round Object, 2011
MDF wood, 198x198x50 cm
Collaboration with Tirza Kater

info

    A round disk is slowly moving trough the exhibition space of ‘de Brakke Grond’ whilst occasionally blocking the visitors passageway. Sometimes a young lady pushes it, and sometimes it is just casually wobbling back and forth on its place. The object shifted its role from object, to sculpture to obstruction in the space. The starting point of making this moving object was to question our relationship to works of art in the space of the exhibition.
